Ihna tlata ashab fi mghamara lektishaf ajmal shawate3 Tounes! Men esh-shrou9 le ghroub esh-shams, nestamta3ou bekoul la7dha bel b7ar

3lech ikhtarnah el-b7ar

El-b7ar diema kan mkanna lil-faragh w l-istirakhah. Min sa7r el-mowj w hawa el-mal7, l'ilbess w 3esh el-hayat, el-b7ar ykhalina nsefru men dounya el-dab.

Rehlitna

Bditna men el-madina l-khadra La Marsa, w planna nzoorou ahla el-shawate3 fi Tounes, bdelin min Mahdia Beach, Djerba Island, w Hammamet.

hdafna

  • Nsta3mlo shawate3 makhloufa w m7ejrin

  • N3amlo dhikrayet ma3a ba3dhna fi kol momen
